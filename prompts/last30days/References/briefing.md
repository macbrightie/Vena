# Briefing Template

Every research run outputs a structured briefing saved here and presented to the user.

## Format

```
## /last30days Briefing
**Topic:** [topic]
**Date:** [date of run]

---

### 🔥 What's Hot Right Now
[Top 3–5 things people are actually talking about — grounded in sources]

### 💬 The Conversation
[What's being debated, what sides people are taking, what's getting upvoted]

### 📌 Strongest Signal
[The single clearest trend or angle with the most cross-source momentum]

### ✏️ Content Angles for Mac
1. [Angle 1 — specific post idea]
2. [Angle 2 — specific post idea]
3. [Angle 3 — specific post idea]

---
*Researched via Reddit, X, YouTube, web — last 30 days*
```

## Storage
Each briefing should also be appended to `history.md` with the date so Mac can look back at past research without re-running.
