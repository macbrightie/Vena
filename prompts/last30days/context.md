# Context — Mac's LinkedIn Niche

## Who I Am
I am a product builder, AI practitioner, and systems thinker. I build software products from idea to launch — and I document the journey. I am not a guru. I am someone actively in the trenches who shares what I learn.

My credibility comes from:
- Building and shipping real products
- Working with founders and agencies hands-on
- Personally testing and living the AI tools, productivity systems, and workflows I talk about

## My Audience
**Primary:** Non-technical founders and professionals who have ideas, funds, and ambition but need help understanding how to build, what tools to use, and how to stay consistent enough to ship.

**Secondary:** Agencies, builders, and early-stage operators who are figuring out how to use AI to move faster and build leaner.

**They are NOT:** Developers looking for deep technical content. Corporate employees climbing a ladder. People who want to be hyped up.

## Topics I Cover
1. **AI tools & software trends** — what's actually worth paying attention to, what's noise
2. **Product building & MVPs** — how non-technical founders can go from idea to launched product
3. **Productivity & accountability systems** — the systems and habits that make shipping possible
4. **The non-technical founder journey** — real stories, real mistakes, real wins

## My Products (Used as Case Studies Only)
I build products and use them as real-world proof points in my content. These are not the focus — I am the focus. Products appear as examples of what's possible, not as pitches.

## Content Filters
When researching, always filter results through this lens:
- Is this relevant to a non-technical founder trying to build something real?
- Is this about AI tools, product, productivity, or the builder journey?
- Is this something people are ACTUALLY debating and discussing — not just news headlines?
- Would a smart, busy founder find this genuinely useful or interesting?

## What to Avoid
- Crypto / Web3 unless directly relevant to product building
- Pure developer / engineering content with no founder angle
- Motivational fluff with no substance
- Anything that feels like hype without evidence
